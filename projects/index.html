Scoresheet

A simple app for keeping score while you play your favourite game. Open Scoresheet instead of grabbing a pencil and sheet of paper. Create a new scoresheet by entering player names. Then after each round of play enter each the score for each player. Scoresheet will sum the score totals for you.

Written entirely in Swift by me. Scoresheet uses CoreData, a View-Interactor-Presenter architecture, and has a funky sketchy look. Enjoy!
